## Goal
Add `*Agg` (alias of `*Aggregate`) to the glossary so users discover the canonical shorthand before encountering compile-time references. Update the Agg.md warning to clarify that `*Aggregate` is the long form and `*Agg` is the canonical shorthand alias.

## Purpose
Users reading glossary or vision first may not know `*Agg` exists until they hit stdlib docs. Documenting the alias relationship in the glossary closes this discoverability gap (issue #154).

## Output

- Updated Agg.md with corrected alias wording
