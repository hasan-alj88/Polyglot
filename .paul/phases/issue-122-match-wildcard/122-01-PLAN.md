<objective>
## Goal
Standardize wildcard catch-all on `*?` in all contexts (verbose and match). Remove the `*`-without-`?` match form so the language has one wildcard syntax everywhere.

## Purpose
NotebookLM audit flagged incomplete cross-references between `*?` and `*`. Rather than document two forms, the user decided `*?` reads as "Otherwise?" and should be the only form. This eliminates the inconsistency at its source.

## Output
Three files updated; EBNF grammar, user-facing docs, and edge-case examples all use `*?` uniformly.
</objective>
