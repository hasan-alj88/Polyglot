## Goal
Fix audience routing so Designers are directed to syntax/grammar content and Architects are directed to runtime/service content, matching their audience guidelines.

The audience-targeting system is undermined when indexes route audiences to content their own guidelines forbid. Fixing this ensures the documentation structure enforces the audience boundaries defined in `docs/audit/audiences/`.

## Output
- `docs/INDEX.md` — "By Audience" section split into Designer and Architect entries
- `docs/technical/INDEX.md` — audience-scoped sections replacing flat listing
- `docs/user/SPEC-INDEX.md` — "For Contributors" split by audience
</objective>
