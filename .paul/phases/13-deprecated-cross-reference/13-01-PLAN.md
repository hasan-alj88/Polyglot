<objective>
## Goal
Add `@d:` (deprecated) as a third typed cross-reference prefix alongside existing `@c:` (concept) and `@u:` (usage).

## Purpose
The `@d:` prefix marks links to content that is deprecated or no longer relevant. This enables the upcoming archive enrichment phases to tag archived docs with clear deprecation markers, making it easy to trace what replaced them and decide what to keep or delete.

## Output
- Updated cross-reference type table in docs/audit/README.md
- Updated conventions and checklist rules
- Updated ref-classification.md with @d: category
</objective>
