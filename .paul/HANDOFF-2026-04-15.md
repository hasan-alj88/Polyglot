## Design Decisions (from this session)

These are already documented in 265-01-PLAN.md and applied to code. Listed here for quick reference:

1. **4 distinct suspended types:** cpu, ram.soft, ram.hard, all
2. **One signal + timing field** (not separate .now/.wait signals)
3. **Free.All stays in suspended set** (no new container)
4. **All operations go through QH** (TM is brain, QH initiates)
5. **Throttle = flag on job hash** (stays in set:executing)
6. **Same resume signal for all types** (QH routes by suspended type)
7. **Snapshot: QH auto-enqueues fork** (atomic operation)
8. **Inspect: Redis state only** (resource metrics from Resource Monitor)
