## Three-Bracket Replacement Rules (for reference)

The 16-step replacement order (symbol reuse requires exact sequence):
1. `{=}` → `{-}` | 2. `[=]` → `(-)` | 3. `[~]` → `(=)` | 4. `[*]` → `(*)`
5. `[>]` → `(>)` | 6. `[<]` → `(<)` | 7. `[p]` → `[=]` | 8. `[r]` → `[-]`
9. `[|]` trigger OR → `[+]` | 10. `[+]` continuation → `[~]`
11. `=>` → `->` (chain only) | 12. `~ForEach`/`~Into` → `=ForEach`/`=Into`
13. Pipeline prefix `=` → `-` | 14. `.~` → `.=` | 15. `%=` → `%-`
16. `@alias=Pipeline` → `@alias-Pipeline`
